Hao Zhang


Background

Hao Zhang is reading MPhil in Land Economy Research in the department of Land Economy, University of Cambridge. She is interested in electric vehicle charging infrastructure. Her research is an aggregate of disciplines in urban design, transport and policy. Hao holds MArch in Urban Design from the Bartlett School of Architecture, UCL and BArch from the Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Hao is a Registered Architect in China and she has more than ten years industrial experience in Beijing.



Research Topic

Understanding the Implications of Electric Vehicles on Car Dependence and Public Transport - an Inter-Sectoral Approach



Research Interest

Electric vehicles, Charging infrastructure, Sustainable mobility
