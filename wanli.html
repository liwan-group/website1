Dr. Li Wan


University Lecturer (Assistant Professor)
Director of the MPhil in Planning, Growth and Regeneration
Co-investigator of Cambridge Centre for Smart Infrastructure and Construction



Background

BArch, MPhil and PhD (Cantab)


Dr Li Wan is an assistant professor at Department of Land Economy, University of Cambridge. He is interested in spatial economic modelling of urban land use and transport. His recent research projects include exploring a social-technical approach for developing novel digital tools for city planning through a series of case studies in Cambridge, and examining the role of micromobility and governance in facilitating the transition towards low-carbon transport.



Research Interest

Land-use and transport modelling; Location and travel choice modelling; Model-based planning policy analysis
